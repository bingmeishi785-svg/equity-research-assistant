# Research Disclaimers And Boundaries

Use disclaimer language when an output resembles investment research, a stock pitch, a valuation memo, or a recommendation.

## Short Disclaimer

This material is for research workflow and educational purposes only. It is not investment advice, a recommendation, or a solicitation to buy or sell any security.

## Data Verification

Before publication or investment use, verify all company facts, market data, financial figures, valuation inputs, and peer comparisons against primary sources or licensed data providers.

## Limitations

- Outputs may rely on user-supplied assumptions.
- Historical performance does not guarantee future results.
- Valuation estimates are sensitive to discount rates, growth, margins, multiples, and terminal assumptions.
- The workflow does not replace professional judgment, compliance review, or regulated financial advice.
