# Payment Setup

Codex can prepare the product files and sales copy, but the project owner must create and control the payment account.

## Simple Launch Path

1. Create a Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Stripe, or GitHub Sponsors account.
2. Create a product named `Equity Research Assistant Pro Pack`.
3. Upload the paid files, such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, sector packs, or a zip archive.
4. Set a starter price, for example `$29` or `$49`.
5. Copy the purchase URL.
6. Add the purchase URL to `README.md` under the Free And Pro Model section.
7. Keep this free GitHub repository public as the trust-building funnel.

## Suggested Product Description

Equity Research Assistant Pro Pack helps analysts and finance students turn company materials into cleaner first-draft research deliverables. It includes editable templates and workflow assets for research reports, valuation models, and stock pitch decks.

## Delivery Checklist

- The free GitHub repository remains usable without payment.
- The paid pack contains assets that are not included in the free repository.
- The sales page states that the product is not investment advice.
- Refund, support, and update policies are visible on the payment page.
- No private workspace files or licensed third-party data are uploaded.
